Microsoft Responsible AI Standard, v2

Activity Group Activity Expected Evidence
Accountability Goals 1.1 Impact assessment 1. Documentation of AI impact assessments conducted.
2. Reports detailing the methodology, findings, and implications of the impact assessment.
3. Evidence of stakeholder engagement in the assessment process.
1.2 Oversight of significant adverse impacts 1. Records of oversight mechanisms implemented to address significant adverse impacts.
2. Documentation of actions taken in response to identified adverse impacts.
3. Evidence of review processes and decision-making related to adverse impacts.
1.3 Fit for purpose 1. Documentation demonstrating alignment of AI systems with intended purposes.
2. Records of evaluations assessing the effectiveness and suitability of AI systems for their intended uses.
3. Evidence of adjustments made to improve the fit-for-purpose of AI systems based on evaluations.
1.4 Data governance and management 1. Documentation of data governance policies and procedures.
2. Records of data management practices, including data collection, storage, processing, and sharing.
3. Evidence of compliance with data protection regulations and standards.
Transparency Goals 2.1 System intelligibility for decision making 1. Documentation of efforts toensure transparency and explainability of AI systems.
2. Records of methodologies used to enhance system intelligibility.
3. Evidence of communication strategies for making AI decisions understandable to stakeholders.
2.2 Communication to stakeholders 1. Records of communication strategies employed to inform stakeholders about AI systems and their impacts.
2. Documentation of stakeholder engagement activities.
3. Evidence of feedback received from stakeholders regarding transparency and communication.
2.3 Disclosure of AI interaction 1. Documentation of policies and procedures for disclosing AI interactions.
2. Records of instances where AI interactions were disclosed to users or affected parties.
3. Evidence of transparency in disclosing AI involvement in decision-making processes.
Fairness Goals 3.1 Quality of service 1. Documentation of measures taken to ensure fairness in the delivery of AI services.
2. Records of evaluations assessing the quality and fairness of AI services.
3. Evidence of adjustments made to improve service quality and fairness.
3.2 Allocation of resources and opportunities 1. Documentation of policies and procedures for resource allocation and opportunity distribution.
2. Records of assessments evaluating the fairness of resource allocation and opportunity distribution.
3. Evidence of efforts to address disparities and biases in resource allocation and opportunity distribution.
3.4 Minimization of stereotyping, demeaning, and erasing outputs 1. Documentation of strategies to minimize stereotypes, demeaning outputs, and erasure in AI systems.
2. Records of assessments identifying and addressing instances of stereotyping and demeaning outputs.
3. Evidence of ongoing efforts to improve the fairness and inclusivity of AI outputs.
Reliability & Safety Goals 4.1 Reliability and safety guidance 1. Documentation of guidelines and standards followed to ensure the reliability and safety of AI systems.
2. Records of risk assessments and mitigation strategies implemented to address reliability and safety concerns.
3. Evidence of adherence to industry best practices for reliability and safety.
4.2 Failures and remediations 1. Records of AI system failures and incidents.
2. Documentation of remediation actions taken to address failures and mitigate their impacts.
3. Evidence of lessons learned and improvements made to prevent similar failures in the future.
4.3 Ongoing monitoring, feedback, and evaluation 1. Documentation of processes for ongoing monitoring, feedback collection, and evaluation of AI systems.
2. Records of monitoring activities, feedback received, and evaluation results.
3. Evidence of continuous improvement efforts based on monitoring and evaluation findings.
Privacy & Security Goals 5.1 Privacy Standard compliance 1. Documentation demonstrating compliance with privacy standards and regulations.
2. Records of privacy assessments and audits conducted.
3. Evidence of measures implemented to protect user privacy in AI systems.
5.2 Security Policy compliance 1. Documentation of compliance with security policies and standards.
2. Records of security assessments and audits performed.
3. Evidence of security controls implemented to safeguard AI systems against threats andbreaches.
Inclusiveness Goal 6.1 Accessibility Standards compliance 1. Documentation of compliance with accessibility standards and guidelines.
2. Records of accessibility assessments conducted.
3. Evidence of efforts to ensure AI systems are accessible to users with diverse abilities and needs.
